Terms & Conditions

1. Membership

All college students are eligible to join the society. Membership requires completion of registration form and payment of annual fees.

2. Code of Conduct

Members must adhere to college rules and maintain decorum during all society activities. Any misconduct may lead to termination of membership.

3. Event Participation

Participation in events is voluntary but encouraged. Some events may require additional fees or registration.

4. Privacy Policy

Member information will be kept confidential and used only for society purposes. Photos from events may be used for promotional materials.

5. Liability

The society is not responsible for any personal injury or loss of property during events. Members participate at their own risk.

6. Amendments

These terms may be updated periodically. Members will be notified of any significant changes.
